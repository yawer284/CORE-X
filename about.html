Our Story at Core-X

Who We Are

Reliable Hardware Online Store

Core‑X started as a small group of hardware enthusiasts who wanted an honest, local place to buy reliable PC parts and fully assembled systems. Over time we grew into a store focused on value, trust, and practical advice.

We deliver fast, transparent service — clear pricing, quick order processing, and dependable after‑sales support. If you prefer, we can also arrange an optional on‑site installation so your new system works perfectly from day one.

Our Vision

Our vision is simple: make high‑performance computing accessible to everyone. We want people to be able to choose, build, or buy a PC that fits their needs and budget — guided by clear advice and real expertise.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer quality components, honest guidance, and full assembly services so everyone — from first‑time builders to seasoned pros — can get a PC that performs and lasts.
